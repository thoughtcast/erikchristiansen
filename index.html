About

I attended the University of British Columbia (Okanagan) where I completed my Bachelor of Arts, majoring in International Relations. Following that, I decided to enter a field where I could take advantage of my technology and design skills. I graduated with my Master of Library and Information Studies from the University of Alberta in 2014. Currently, I work as an Assistant Professor / Librarian at Mount Royal University in Calgary, Alberta.

prefer to think big picture and long-term. During my academic and professional career, I've had the incredible fortune to serve in a variety of different roles. I continue to maintain a willingness to learn and I am always working on a project or taking a course to further develop my skills and pursue my interests.
