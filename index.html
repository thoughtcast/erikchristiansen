About

I’m part of the first cohort enrolled in the University of Alberta’s joint business and library and information studies masters program. My goal is to utilize my background in art history, and my experience working at the Alberta government library and conducting information policy research, to attain a career within an academic or special library, government body, or cultural institution.


Demonstrated strengths:

• Educational background in, and continued personal commitment to, the history of art.
• Professional knowledge of library operations and collection management practices, standards and software.
• Extensive primary and secondary research experience and written and oral communication skills.
• Experience training, supervising and closely collaborating with colleagues
