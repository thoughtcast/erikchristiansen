About

I'm originally from Canada's California - Vancouver Island. I can still smell that ocean air...

My longtime interest in politics led me to the world of academe. I completed a Bachelor of Arts majoring in International Relations at the University of British Columbia. My fascination with information technology and higher education led me to the University of Alberta where I did my Master of Library and Information Studies. Currently, I work as an Assistant Professor / Librarian at Mount Royal University, where I teach information literacy, do research, and have fun with my colleagues.
