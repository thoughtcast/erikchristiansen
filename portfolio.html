Web Projects and Services

2015 International Insect Hormones Workshop

  • This biological sciences conference took place in Crete, in July 2015. I was the project lead, and I designed both the site's structure and created all graphic design elements. In addition, I worked closely with our central IT department to integrate a new campus online payment and registration platform. The website was built on the Drupal 7 CMS.

MediaCore - University of Alberta

  • Launching this multimedia streaming platform was a collaborative effort between myself and colleagues from the Faculties of Education, and Medicine and Dentistry at the University of Alberta. I was deeply involved in the design and implementation of the training, as well as many of the information architecture aspects.

Tech Bytes

  • I founded Tech Bytes in the summer of 2013 as a personal side project, and it has now become a much larger enterprise that is ad supported. Here I write trends in the mobile computing space (including smartphones, tablets, wearables, and the Internet of Things). It is an example of my love for both technology and writing.

SLIS Capping Project

  • I created this website as part of my final capping project for my Masters of Library and Information Studies, at the University of Alberta. It outlines some of my coursework, work experience, and professional engagement while in the program.
